Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Cheese blend
  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add pasta noodles
  3. Boil pasta for 10 minutes
  4. Drain pasta water
  5. in a different pot, add the heavy whipping cream and cheese blend. Constanly stir so it does not burn
  6. once the cream and cheese mixture thicken pour the macaroni noodles into the cheese mix.
  7. Pour this mixture into your baking dish and place baking dish in oven for 350 for 10 minutes
  8. remove from the oven, let cool and serve.
