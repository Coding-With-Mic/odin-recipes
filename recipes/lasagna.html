Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Preferred Pasta Sauce
  • Cheese of choice
  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add pasta noodles
  3. Boil pasta for 10 minutes
  4. Drain pasta water
  5. Alternate layering the boiled noodles with the sauce and cheese into a baking dish.
  6. place baking dish in oven for 350 for 20 minutes
  7. remove from the oven, let cool and serve.
