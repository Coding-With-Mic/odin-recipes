Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Preferred Pasta Sauce
  • (optional) Parmesan Cheese
  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add pasta noodles
  3. Boil pasta for 10 minutes
  4. Drain pasta water
  5. Add chosen pasta sauce
  6. Mix together well
  7. (optional) top with parmesan cheese
