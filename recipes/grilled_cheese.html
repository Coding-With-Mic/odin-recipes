Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese of choice
  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. place desired cheese between 2 pieces of bread of choice.
  3. Once butter is melted, lay the bread down in the pan.
  4. Check the bread that is toasting. Once golden brown, flip
  5. Once the other side is golden brown remove from pan.
  6. place baking dish in oven for 350 for 20 minutes
  7. Slice and serve.
